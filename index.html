  • Analyzed booking data to identify that most trips are round trips, with circle and one-way trips being less frequent, and that solo travelers or pairs are the majority.
  • Discovered that flights are more common on weekdays, especially on Day 1, indicating higher demand compared to weekends.
  • Found that Australia leads in bookings (39.1%), with Malaysia (15.7%) and South Korea (10.0%) following, while other countries contribute smaller shares.
  • Identified that approximately 85% of bookings are incomplete, highlighting a significant drop-off or issues in the booking process.
  • Investigated correlations, revealing weak relationships between booking_complete and other factors, with notable correlations including a slight positive one with wants_extra_baggage and a negative one with flight_duration.
  • Utilized OneHotEncoder, StandardScaler, RandomForestClassifier, and XGBClassifier, achieving an 87% accuracy in predicting booking completions through comprehensive modeling and hyperparameter tuning.
